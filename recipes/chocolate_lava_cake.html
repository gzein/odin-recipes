Chocolate Lava Cake

Description

A simple recipe of a chocolate lava cake. 'nuff said.
Serves for two.

Ingredients

  • 85g of bittersweet chocolate, 70-75% cacao, chopped
  • 3 tbspn unsalted butter, cut into cubes + more for the ramekin
  • 3 tbspn granulated sugar + more for the ramekin
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 tspn vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tspn salt
  • 2 tbspn all-purpose flour
  • Confectioner's sugar, to serve (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Heat oven to 425 degrees and butter a 10-ounce ramekin. Dust the buttered ramekin with granulated sugar.
    2. Combine the chocolate and 3 tbspn butter in a heat-safe bowl set over a pan of simmering water. Alternatively, microwave in 30s blasts.
    3. In a medium bowl, combine 3 tbspn sugar, egg, egg yolk, vanilla and salt. Whisk vigorously until mix is thick, foamy and pale, about 2 mins. Whisk in the flour until smooth.
    4. Using a spatula, add the chocolate to the egg mix and stir gently until all combined.
    5. Pour the mix into the ramekin. bake for 12-14 mins or until the eges are set and puffed, but the center is still soft when lightly pressed.
    6. Use an offset spatula or small knife to loosen the edges of the cake from the ramekin. Place a plate over the ramekin and carefully invert the warm cake. Use an oven mitt or clean kitchen towl to remove the ramekin, dust the cake with confectioner's sugar and serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
